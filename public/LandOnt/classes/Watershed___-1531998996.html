    Watershed. All the land surface that sheds its water to a
    given discharge point. Since the area of a watershed is all
    the area that drains to a given point in a drainageway, and
    water quantity and quality is an important environmental
    variable to manage, the watershed is often the most reasonable
    and effective landscape management unit."
