    • A datum is a basic element used as a reference for other component elements in design.
    • A point can act as a center around which elements are arranged or from where they are controlled.
    • A line is commonly used as a datum in a variety of ways, both real and implied.
    • A plane provides a reference for the position of other elements.
    • Solid and open volumes act in similar fashion to points and planes.
    • Time can act as a datum for events and change in the landscape.
