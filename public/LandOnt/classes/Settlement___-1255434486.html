Class: Settlement

Annotations (1)

  • rdfs:comment "La
    settlement 1. The sinking of earth, the fall in
    elevation of a ground surface in a backfilled
    trench or excavation, if not properly compacted
    or caused by underground erosion. 2. The slow
    downward movement or change in elevation of
    a structure that is slightly sinking because of a
    poor bearing subsurface, or poor compaction
    beneath it, etc. 3. An agreed upon compromise.
    4. The downward movement of solids in a liquid
    such as water due to gravity. When flowing
    water carrying sediment slows, it deposits material
    through this process."
